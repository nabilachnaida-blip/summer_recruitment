Summer Recruitment Program

Our Summer Recruitment Program is a unique opportunity for motivated candidates to join Stellantis Morocco during one of our largest recruitment campaigns of the year.

Unlike traditional recruitment processes, this campaign is designed to simplify access to employment by allowing candidates to participate directly in interview sessions without an initial CV pre-selection.

Whether you are a recent graduate, an experienced technician, or looking for a new professional challenge, this program gives you the opportunity to demonstrate your motivation, technical skills, and potential directly to our recruitment teams.

Successful candidates will benefit from a structured recruitment process, personalized support, and the opportunity to join one of the most innovative automotive companies in the world.

Don't miss this opportunity to accelerate your career and become part of the Stellantis family.
